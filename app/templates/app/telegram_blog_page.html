Telegram


Telegram Messenger is a globally accessible freemium, cross-platform, encrypted, cloud-based and centralized instant messaging service.





How It Works

Create a webhook in your dashboard, Attach the Webhook to your TradingView alerts together with the message that you would want to be posted on your Telegram account on the message box of the alert. You can send your alerts to Telegram Channels, Groups, and private chats. You can also switch between different channels and groups at any time.






Features

This automation tool lets you forward TradingView alerts to your Telegram Channels or Groups. You can also use Markdown and HTML to customize your messages.

You can also switch your alerts to different Telegram channels and groups at any time.






Alert Message Example

BUY {{ticker}} e= {{close}}, tp= 1.001, sl= 1.0009, q= 0.1, m=77 link=https://www.example.com/image-to-send-to-telegram-with-message

Details: e is the price at which you want to enter your trade. tp is the target price for profit. sl is the price at which you want to limit losses. q is the amount of the asset to trade. m is a unique identifier for the trade.






Modify Trade

m: 77, tp: 0.001, sl: Breakeven (move stop loss to breakeven condition)




Close Trade

m: 77

Closes the order
